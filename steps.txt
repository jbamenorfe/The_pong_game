The PONG GAME
Follow the following steps to develop the pong GAME

(1) Create the screen
(2) Create and move the first paddle
(3) Create and move the second paddle
(4) Create the ball and make it move
(5) Detect the ball's collision with the wall and bounce it
(6) Detect the ball's collision with the paddle
(7) Detect when the paddle misses the ball
(8) Keep the score
